# Demo Outreach Plan (Show to Target People)

This plan operationalizes the "show it to accountants" validation requirement.

## Target People

1. Independent accountants serving freelancers/SMEs
2. Small accounting firms with B2B clients
3. Fractional CFO/bookkeeping consultants

## Contact List Template

Track at least 30 targets:
- 15 independent accountants
- 10 accounting firms
- 5 CFO/bookkeeping consultants

For each contact record:
- Name
- Firm
- Email / LinkedIn
- Segment
- Contact date
- Demo date
- Reaction score (1-5)
- Recommendation likelihood (Yes/No)

## Validation Script (15 minutes)

1. Problem framing (2 min)
   - "Clients are owed legal late-payment charges but rarely claim them."
2. Live product walkthrough (7 min)
   - Fill form
   - Calculate
   - Generate PDF
   - Preview email
   - Send
3. Feedback prompts (6 min)
   - "Is this useful for your clients?"
   - "Would you recommend this?"
   - "What would block adoption?"
   - "Would clients pay for this?"

## Success Criteria

Validation is successful when at least:
- 60% say "useful"
- 40% say "would recommend"
- 20% ask pricing or pilot questions

## 2-Week Outreach Cadence

- Week 1: Contact 30 people, run 10 demos
- Week 2: Run remaining 10 demos and summarize findings

## Deliverables to Share

- Frontend demo URL
- One sample PDF output
- One sample email preview
- 1-page product explainer (from README)
