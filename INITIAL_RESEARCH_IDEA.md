# Statutory Interest Autopilot for Late Invoices in the EU

## Candidate shortlisting and final pick

Across Europe, late B2B payments are a recurring, financially urgent problem for non-tech SMEs (trades, agencies, wholesalers, clinics, small manufacturers).

### Key signals

- ~47% of companies report issues due to late payments  
- ~70% don’t claim interest or compensation  
- SMEs avoid claiming due to fear of damaging relationships  

➡️ This creates a strong opportunity:  
**value already exists in law, but isn’t captured**

## Core idea

### “EU Late Payment Rights Autopilot”

A system that:
1. Calculates statutory interest + €40 fee  
2. Generates official notice  
3. Sends staged emails  
4. Tracks outcomes  
5. Prepares escalation pack  

👉 Key insight:  
**“Blame the system, not the person”**
